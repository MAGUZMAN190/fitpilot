Your personal gym co-pilot — built to help you stay consistent, track progress, and hit your fitness goals.

🚀 What is FitPilot?

FitPilot is a free, personal gym tracker that lives entirely in your browser. No subscriptions, no bloat — just a clean tool to log your workouts, build routines, and watch your streaks grow.

Your data is synced to your Google account via Firebase, so it's always available on any device you sign into — phone, tablet, or computer.

☁️ Cloud Sync 📵 No Ads 🔒 Private 💸 Free

✨ Features

  • 🏠
    Dashboard — See your streak, total workouts, and missed days at a glance.
  • 📚
    Exercise Library — 50+ exercises across all muscle groups with tips and default sets/reps.
  • 📋
    Custom Routines — Build and save your own workout routines to reuse any day.
  • 📅
    Calendar View — Log workouts to specific days, mark rest days, and track your history month by month.
  • ✨
    Routine Generator — Instantly generate a workout for any muscle group with one tap.
  • 🏅
    Achievements — Earn badges as you hit milestones like streaks, total workouts, and more.
  • ☁️
    Cloud Save — Sign in with Google and your data follows you everywhere.

📖 How to Use

1. Log a workout — Go to the Calendar, tap any day, and add exercises or assign a saved routine.

2. Build routines — Head to Routines, tap the builder, pick your exercises, and save it with a name.

3. Track your streak — Work out on consecutive days to build your streak. Missing a day resets it, so stay consistent!

4. Your data is safe — Everything syncs automatically to your Google account. Export a backup anytime from your Profile page.
